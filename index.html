Set: Hover and press your keybind

Clear: Hover and press Escape

Lock / Unlock: Hover and right click

Press CLEAR to clear all unlocked buttons

All binds are saved to browser local storage

Supported kebyinds:

  • All characters A-Z, 0-9, -, =
  • Mouse 3, 4, Wheel Up, Wheel Down
  • Shift, Control, Alt modifiers
  • For CTRL+T, you must use ALT+CTRL+T to bind it

MOUSE4 (M4) will work, however it will go back a page in your browser. Simply come back to this page and it will be saved
